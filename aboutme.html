About Me

I'm a game programmer, as well as bit of a designer. Creating video games has been a part of my daily life for the past three years, ever since I started. I'm currently in the development of the Blue Flame engine and demo game M.E.C.C., made in C++ and OpenGl with 3 other members for my capstone project at Humber College. I've also been in development of my own 3D game engine, the Cryographic Engine, to help further advance my knowledge of OpenGl. I've enjoyed these game development years more than any other, because I've learnt how to create the things I've loved my entire life. Playing them as long I can remember, it's hard to imagine my life differently. Some of the great games like Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time and Super Smash Bros come to mind. My life goal is to make games like the ones in my childhood, to help impact someone else just like I was.

Thanks for visiting!
